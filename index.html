Intro

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a data professional with expertise in data wrangling, project management, process improvement, and automation using Python. Throughout my career, I have been able to apply these skills to deliver innovative solutions that drive business growth and improve overall efficiency.

Keep updated with my latest thoughts and musings on the world of data and technology. Check out my blog for more information.

Work

In my previous role as a Data Analyst, I was responsible for collecting, cleaning, and transforming large datasets for use in business intelligence and analytics applications. I used Python to automate this process, reducing the time required from weeks to just a few hours. Additionally, I implemented process improvements to streamline data delivery and ensure accuracy and completeness of data.

Another project I worked on involved leading a team of data professionals to implement a new project management system for a global corporation. My team used Python to automate key processes, reducing manual effort and increasing efficiency. We also implemented a new data tracking system that helped improve visibility into project progress and allowed for real-time tracking of key metrics.

In my current role as a Digital Content Manager, I engage in Data Strategy for content and process improvement.

About

I hold a Bachelor's in Computer Science and an MBA in Organizational Behavior and Management of Information Systems. I bring several years of data experience and project management to the table, as well as expertise in data wrangling and automation using Python. For the past 10 years, I have been working remotely, harnessing technology to drive business growth and efficiency. With today's technologies, talent is no longer confined by location and I am eager to utilize my skills to help organizations succeed.
